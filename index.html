Industrial OMR Specifications & Knowledge Base

Fiducials (Anchors)

The four 'L' shaped markers at the corners are crucial for the optical scanner to detect the orientation and scale of the sheet. They act as the coordinate origin (0,0) for image processing algorithms.

Dropout Colors

We use #ff003c (Pantone 192 C). High-end OMR scanners use red light to drop out this specific color, leaving only the black marks (pencil/pen) visible to the sensor. This dramatically improves reading accuracy.

Timing Tracks

The vertical ladder on the left edge synchronizes the scanner's reading speed with the row position. Without this, a paper feed error could offset the reading of an entire column.

Paper Physics

This generator simulates standard A4 (210mm x 297mm) GSM 100 paper. For best results, print at 100% scale without margins. The layout automatically adjusts grid density to fit the page without overcrowding.
